Virtual Therapy

VirtualTx, founded by the same owners as RemoteSpeech, an all-round teletherapy service's intelligent platform, providing virtual therapy services option since 2015 and has helped many schools and higher education programs to deliver online therapy at the highest level. VirtualTx is an all new cloud-based web application and web portal, an intelligent platform developed and powered by RemoteSpeech’s 20+ years of experience in providing optimal therapy & solutions. VirtualTx’s goal is to optimize and provide one-stop-solution for therapy with all fully customized solutions and services as per therapist's needs.

What we Provide

An all-round therapy solution for clients and therapists as they enjoy a library of therapy materials while also being able to take care of the business needs that are required of busy therapists - video capability, scheduling, notes, billing and much more!
